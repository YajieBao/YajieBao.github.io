About Me

I am currently a final-year Ph.D. student in Statistics at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. Before coming to SJTU, I received my B.S. degree in Statistics from East China Normal University. I have been working with Mingrui Liu on federated learning and deep learning theory since November 2021. At SJTU, I'm a member of Haojie Ren's research group on conformal inference. I was fortunate to visit Prof. Changliang Zou's group at Nankai University from April-August, 2023.

Research interests
